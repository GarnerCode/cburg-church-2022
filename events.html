Events

We host a variety of events throughout the year, and would love for you to join us! Below are just a few of our major events!

Vacation Bible School

Bring your friends and family to our three-night-long Vacation Bible School, where kids learn about God and the bible through fun interactive activities such as reenactments of stories from the bible, arts and crafts, singing, and more!

Canned Food Drive

Join in our annual scavenger hunt for canned foods! We split up into groups and travel Callisburg collecting can food donations from local residents and donate them to the local food bank so that the less fortunate can enjoy fresh meals!

Men's Fishing Trip

Take a relaxing trip out to Lake Texoma for our annual weekend-long Men's Fishing Trip! Come catch some fish with us and enjoy a fish fry at the end of the weekend!
