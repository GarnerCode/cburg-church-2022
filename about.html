About Us

We are a small country church with a large heart for the Lord and others. Our goal is to reach as many people with the message of Christ as we can from our little corner of the world. We would love to see you and invite you to come spend some time with us!

Services

Our Sunday morning services begin at 10:30am, followed by our evening services at 6:00pm, as well as Wednesday evening services at 7:00pm! All of our services are livestreamed to YouTube and Facebook, and we'd love for you to tune in!

Sunday School

Sunday School classes begin at 9:30am, and we have available preschool, elementary school, middle and high school, and adult classes!

Annual Events

We host a wide variety of events throughout the year, and welcome you to come spend time with us!
